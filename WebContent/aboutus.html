About Us

Our History

Started in 2010, Telph quickly established itself as a pan-India integrated GSM operator offering 2G and 3G services, and has its own NLD and ILD operations, and ISP license. Telph has won spectrum to launch 4G services across 10 key markets and has initiated multiple steps towards introduction of 4G LTE services on 1800 MHz, in a phased manner from calendar year 2016 onwards.

Telph has been a pioneer in introducing customized product offerings for segmented customers. It is the first mobile operator to introduce innovative value added services in the Indian telephony market, and has remained ahead of the industry in data product offerings. Driving the growth of mobile broadband in India, Telph services over 37.2 million data users (as on Q1FY16). Today, data contributes over 17.7% of Telph’s service’s revenue.
